Bio

I'm a PhD candidate at Université Côte d'Azur, my work is carried out at the lab i3s in Sophia Antipolis, France, co-supervised by Fabrice Huet (SCALE team) and Guillaume Urvoy-Keller (Signet Team).

I obtained my Master's degree at Università di Bologna in Italy in 2017. In the academic year 2016/2017, thanks to the Erasmus+ project, I was able to follow the Master 2 Ubinet at Université Nice Sophia Antipolis. Where I had the opportunity for a 6-month research internship in the i3s laboratory.

Research

PhD Thesis

My thesis is about Joint Application and Network Optimization of Big Data Analytics, where we study the network's behaviour when using BigData applications. After an initial wide analysis of the spectrum, we aim to exploit the network layer to optimize the upper middleware and application layer.
We are currently focusing on Data Stream Processing with Storm.
